How to Use the Similarity Matcher

Simply type or paste in the two text bodies you want to compare in each of the two boxes and click on compare

How it Works

The similarity score you receive is based on the following algorithms:

  • Cosine
  • Jaccard Index
  • TF_IDF
  • Long Monge Elkan : Using Levenshtein Scoring and Synonym Checking

Furthermore, you will also be able to see a summary of both of the texts.
